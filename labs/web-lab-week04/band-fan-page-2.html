Skillet's Musical Style

As previously mentioned, Skillet is an American Christian rock band. Critics also label Skillet’s musical style under the following genres; Christian metal, Alternative Rock, Hard Rock, Metal, and Symphonic Metal. Generally, their music style is a combination of these genres.

Cooper’s idea of the “perfect music” is one that can unite individuals spiritually and socially. Every song the band has produced is created under this ideal.
