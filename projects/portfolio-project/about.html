About Me

Hello! My name is Benjamin Dashiell. I am a junior at the University of Colorado Boulder, majoring in Computer Science (Engineering) and a minor in the Creative, Technology, Design program (CTD). I work as a video editor for Leed’s School of Business.

Outside of the college and work, I spend my free time working on creative projects. I primarily focus on video editing, but I occasionally do photography and side coding projects. I currently edit and manage two YouTube channels, DragonLordLeda (my personal channel) and Just More Gamers (a collaborative gaming channel). I can’t explain why but I’ve always had the drive to create.

Why the name, DragonLordLeda?

It all started when I hatched from an egg as a dragon. Okay, I’m joking.

The story behind the name is back when I played Clash of Clans, I wanted to change my player name to something more fitting. I’ve always liked dragons as a kid and one of the player names I wrote down was DragonLordL10A. The L10A was an abbreviation of an old username (that's a whole other explanation). I don’t know why 14 year old thought having it in my player names was cool. Anyway, I showed my friend these player names to see what he thought was good. My handwriting was SO BAD that he misread the L10A as “Lida” and pronounced it as Lee-da. We laughed about how dumb that was but eventually, it stuck with me. Since then, DragonLordLeda has went from my player name to becoming my creative persona.

Also no, I don’t play Clash of Clans anymore :/
